Who We Are

Road Fit was started by touring professionals who got tired of wellness advice that assumed you had a kitchen, a gym, and a consistent sleep schedule. We're FOH engineers, lighting directors, tour managers, and musicians writing for people who understand the life.

What We Cover

Mental health and loneliness. Nutrition and fitness on the road. Making friends and maintaining relationships. Professional communication. Finding purpose and happiness when your life doesn't look like anyone else's. All of it, honestly.
